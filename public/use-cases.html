The Challenge

A growing fashion retailer was struggling with manual order processing, leading to delays, errors, and poor customer satisfaction. They were processing 500+ orders daily across multiple platforms but losing customers due to slow fulfillment.

  • Manual order entry taking 10-15 minutes per order
  • Inventory discrepancies causing overselling
  • Customers not receiving timely shipping updates
  • High error rate in order fulfillment (8%)

The Solution

We implemented a comprehensive e-commerce automation workflow that connected their Shopify store, inventory management system, shipping providers, and customer communication tools.
